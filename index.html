About the Author

Beryl Nerissa Sutherland Murdock, affectionately known as Ms. B or Mrs. Murdock, is a mother, step mom, grandma and dear friend to many. She pursued a career in teaching and dedicated her life to educating children in the communities of Sandy Hill, Grateful Hill, Above Rocks in St. Catherine and also in Shirley Castle and Mount Hermon in Portland. She battled and overcame stage 3 cancer at the tender age of 84 and has since been featured in many of her grandchildren's celebratory tweets.
